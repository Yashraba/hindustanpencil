NATARAJ

Nataraj, the first brand of Hindustan Pencils was launched
with a brand philosophy of pencils for all, thus carrying a
value for money image.

Apsara, launched in 1970s as a premium stationery
brand started with drawing pencils as its first product.
With the launch of writing pencils and other allied
stationery products in 1990's, Apsara has grown to be
the most preferred scholastic brand

Hindustan Pencils started manufacturing pens in the year
2007. Nataraj Classic Ball Pen, with its famous Nataraj red
and black design is now one of the most visible pens in
India.
